AC Regimen (q3w x 4 cycles)

Pre-medications (30 mins before chemotherapy)

  • Dexamethasone 20mg IV
  • Ondansetron 16mg IV
  • Ranitidine 50mg IV
  • Diphenhydramine 50mg IV

Drug Administration

  • Doxorubicin 60mg/m²
    • Dilute in 50ml NS
    • Administer as slow IV push over 3-5 minutes
    • Use side-port of freely running NS
  • Cyclophosphamide 600mg/m²
    • Dilute in 250ml NS
    • Infuse over 30 minutes

Post-medications (Days 2-4)

  • Dexamethasone 8mg PO BID
  • Ondansetron 8mg PO TID prn
  • Metoclopramide 10mg PO TID prn

Supportive Care

  • Consider G-CSF support as per guidelines
  • Antiemetic prophylaxis (Highly emetogenic)
  • Baseline and periodic cardiac monitoring
  • Monitor CBC, LFT weekly for first cycle

Carboplatin-Paclitaxel Regimen (q3w)

Pre-medications (30-60 mins before chemotherapy)

  • Dexamethasone 20mg IV
  • Diphenhydramine 50mg IV
  • Ranitidine 50mg IV
  • Ondansetron 16mg IV

Pre-hydration

  • NS 500ml over 30 minutes

Drug Administration

  • Paclitaxel 175mg/m²
    • Dilute in 500ml NS
    • Infuse over 3 hours
    • Monitor for hypersensitivity in first 15-30 mins
  • Carboplatin AUC 5-6
    • Dilute in 250ml D5W
    • Infuse over 30-60 minutes

Post-medications

  • Ondansetron 8mg PO BID x 2-3 days prn
  • Metoclopramide 10mg PO TID prn

Supportive Care

  • Monitor CBC weekly for first cycle
  • Check creatinine before each cycle
  • Monitor for neuropathy
  • Consider G-CSF support if indicated
